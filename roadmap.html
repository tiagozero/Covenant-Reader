The Method
What is Refold?
Refold is a language acquisition method built on one core insight: you acquire language by understanding it, not by studying it. Grammar drills and memorization build shallow knowledge. Comprehensible input builds deep, native-like fluency.
🧠 How Acquisition Works
Your brain has a built-in language acquisition device. Feed it enough comprehensible input — content you mostly understand — and it will subconsciously internalize grammar, vocabulary, and pronunciation without explicit study. This is how you learned your first language, and it works for your second too.

⚙️ The Three Core Activities
Active Immersion
Focused reading or listening with full attention. Look up words. Mine into Léxico & Anki. 30–60 min/day.
Passive Listening
Target language audio in the background — while commuting, cooking, exercising. 1–2 hr/day.
Active Study
Anki reviews, grammar survey, core vocab. Keep it short — the real learning happens in immersion.
📚 Resource Types in This Roadmap
📘 AssimilStory-based bilingual dialogues with audio. Best all-rounder for Refold — input-first, natural progression.
🎙️ LinguaPhoneAudio-first listen-repeat method. Excellent for ear training, phonetics, and passive replay.
🌿 Natural MethodMeaning-first, no translation. Closest in philosophy to Refold — target language from day one.
📖 Graded Reader+AudioPre-calibrated to comprehension levels. Ideal for sentence mining and read+listen immersion in Stage 2A.
📻 Learner PodcastPodcasts designed for learners. Perfect for passive listening and on-the-go immersion in Stages 0–2B.
🎬 TV · Cartoon · MovieNative visual media. Starts as easy/subtitled in Stage 2A; transitions to unsubtitled native content in 2C+.
📝 Grammar StudyClassic grammar textbooks. Used sparingly — one survey pass in Stage 1, targeted reference thereafter.
🎧 Pod101 optionalStructured video/audio lessons. Optional supplement — useful for grammar overviews and cultural context.
Léxico is your immersion companion. It highlights words as you browse, lets you mine sentences directly from native content into your Anki deck, and tracks your vocabulary growth across your Refold journey — all without leaving your browser.
